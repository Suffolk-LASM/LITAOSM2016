QnA Markup

QnA is a markup language for people with little or no programming experience. It was designed with attorneys in mind and transforms blocks of text into interactive question and answer sessions (QnAs). These QnAs can be used as stand-alone expert systems or in the aid of rule-based document construction. See example below. Plus, they can be fun, and the entire project is open source. Among other things, that means free.
